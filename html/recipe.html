all natural cookies and cream cocoa

ingredients

  • organic caocao chocolate mix
  • taro milk tea powder
  • creamer(powdered or milk)
  1. add milk or water to pot and set to slow boil
  2. when heated, add chocolate and taro powder, slowly stirring it in
  3. add additional creamer to taste (if using water this is where you add powdered milk)
  4. reduce heat to simmer and serve warmed
